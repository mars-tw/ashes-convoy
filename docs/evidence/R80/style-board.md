# R80 style board

- Direction: a patched mobile ash shelter, not a generic cozy bedroom and not neon sci-fi.
- Palette: rust brown `#8b4b24`, ember amber `#d88a42`, cold steel `#607485`, near-black `#111014`, worn canvas `#8b7256`.
- Signature contrast: cold ruined highway or hostile environment outside, restrained amber practical light inside or on the convoy.
- Material language: riveted steel, soot, repair seams, scratched floor, patched canvas, rain or ash depth.
- Xi identity: exactly one seated girl; short dark-brown hair, brown scarf or shawl, dark trousers and boots.
- Runtime layout: portrait 13:15, open central aisle, bed on the right, heater and Xi on the lower-left, clear wall/window/floor furniture anchors.
- Promo layout: opaque 16:9, one readable environment-specific convoy vehicle in the lower third, no text, UI, logo, border, watermark, foreground person, duplicate vehicle, neon or glossy photography.

References and SHA-256 values are recorded in `room-art-manifest.json` and `docs/promo/manifest.json`.
